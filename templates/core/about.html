About Us

Rooted in tradition, driven by quality — our journey from garden to cup.

Who We Are

Based in the vibrant hub of Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, A M S Tea Traders is a trusted manufacturer, blender, and wholesale supplier of premium tea products. We specialize in sourcing high-grade tea leaves directly from India’s most celebrated tea-growing terrains, carefully processing them to cater to both commercial businesses and household kitchens across the region.

Our Vision

"To bridge the gap between India’s finest pristine tea gardens and everyday consumers by delivering uncompromised quality, exceptional strength, and authentic regional flavors in every single cup."

Our Sourcing & Mastery

Sourcing directly from India's most celebrated terrains with master processing.

🍃

The Assam Heritage

We procure rich, robust, and malty Assam tea leaves, chosen specifically for their deep color and invigorating, full-bodied strength.

🏔️

The Nilgiri Freshness

Located near the mist-covered Western Ghats, we source fresh, fragrant South Indian leaf and dust tea varieties known for their brisk flavor and distinct aroma.

⚙️

CTC Processing Excellence

Utilizing advanced Crush, Tear, Curl (CTC) manufacturing methods, we ensure our tea grains release maximum flavor, uniform color, and optimum consistency when brewed.
